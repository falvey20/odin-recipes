Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an Italian dish made with pasta, beef and vegetables.

It's an easy meal to make that can be enjoyed by the whole family.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Beef Mince
  • Carrots
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Seal the Beef
  3. Add to tray
  4. Bake for 30mins
